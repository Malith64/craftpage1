About us

Welcome to Craft by boby! Nestled in the heart of Kandy, Sri Lanka, we're artisans who pour passion into every handmade creation-from cozy handcrafted slippers and lovingly hand-painted handloom sarees to thoughtfully curated birthday gift sets, rings, earrings, and more. Our mission is to bring unique, high-quality crafts to all corners of Sri Lanka, sharing our cultural heritage and creativity with you.
